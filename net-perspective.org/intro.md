
# Net Perspective


**defn:** Net Perspective
*noun*

An idea, a protocol, and a group to support the first two.
net-perspective.org

---

**defn:** prspct
*noun, pronounced "perspect"* 

The reference implementation of that protocol.

---

**defn:** context
*noun*

e.g.: #comp.lang.c

Consider them as hierarchical hashtags:
#comp.lang is a parent context of #comp.lang.c

---

**defn:** relate
*noun, verb*

Relates are publishable direct links between entities, specifically a "subject" and
"object" entity, *mapping contexts between the entities*.

```
subject-entity#food.treasure -> object-entity#food.trash
```

I relate my `#food.treasure` to your `#food.trash`.

Relates are created and controlled by the subject.

---

**defn:** publication
*noun*

A set of relates belonging to an entity that is shared.

---

**defn:** perspective
*noun*

An entity combines their publication, with the publications of those they relate to, to produce a perspective:
a set of object entities grouped by context.

---

The idea:
- You are in control. No spam, scammers, etc, only wrong relations.
- A decentralised category system. How you organise things is your own business.
- Small evolvable spec and code. Applications built on top.
- Primitives that can be combined in imaginative ways, allow natural social trust systems to form.
- More "information tool" than "social media".
- Simple data, easy to migrate to/from, easy to interconnect.
