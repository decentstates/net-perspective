## Anti-spam/scam

Emergent arbitrary whitelists/allowlists of uris or emails, to be used as you see fit.

Want to only allow people in a perspective to your website? Use a list of allowed emails.
That is letting bots in now? You can trace where the bots are coming from and fix the issue.

The key point is auditability, and the fact you only have to manage 5-10 people in a perspective.
